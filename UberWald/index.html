UberWald is an MMO survival-settlement builder rpg set in the infinite twisting paths of a haunted forest. Players control survivors who wandered too far and too deep into forests of our world and ended up somewhere much darker. Here the sun emerges one day in seven. Here the nightmares of humanity's legends creep, lurk, and hunt. Will you build a bastion for humanity to survive in? Join the creatures of the night? Carve out a secluded hermitage? Whatever your choice, be wary. The forest will change around you...
