Occupy your mind, not your time

UberWald respects your time. We all love being able to progress through a rich game, especially with friends. But progression shouldn't cost hours upon hours of grinding. We believe the key to satisfying gameplay is in meaningful decision making and lasting relationships between players. UberWald is designed for a maximum of 10 minutes of gameplay per day to achieve the same level of satisfaction players can get from playing other MMOs full time.
